Students and instructor will have a short conversation at beginning of class to establish etiquette and expectations together

The first week will be focused on exploring different types of video games through play and discussion. We will also create short "animated stories" with Stencyl at first with no interactivity and adding interactive elements as programming concepts are introduced.

In the second week we will brainstorm final projects, review tough programming concepts, build and playtest games. Each day a different group of students will present the concepts behind their games to class focusing on the story elements and the gaming experience.
